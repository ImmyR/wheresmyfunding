About us

Show me the funding is a web application designed to display where and how research funding is dispersed across the United Kingdom. By searching for topics of interest, you will be able to view related past and current research projects, where the lead institutions for those projects are taking place on a map along with appropriate related information, and see connections between institutions working together on projects.

Getting started

Using this app is as simple as typing your topic of interest in the search box and hitting submit! We'll then search for any relevant projects and, if there are any matches, we'll show them to you...
